The story of you

A love letter to the way you move through the world.

Grace and spark

You make ordinary moments feel cinematic. The way you tilt your head when you think, your soft laugh when you catch me staring — it’s all the proof I need that magic is real.

Kindness in action

Your kindness doesn’t speak loudly; it shows up consistently. In texts that say “get home safe,” in coffees made just right, in the quiet courage to keep choosing love.

Your smile, my sunrise

There’s a way the room changes when you enter — like someone turned the dimmer to dawn. You are my favorite view.

I choose you

In every small decision and every big dream, it’s you. Today and all the days after.

I love the way you light up rooms you don’t even know you’re brightening. Happy birthday, my love. May we always find our way back to this feeling.

Forever yours,

— Your Name
